Senior Design Capstone

Football Shoe Protective System

Designed a lightweight impact-protection system for soccer cleats using a Nomex honeycomb core filled with shear-thickening fluid (STF) and encapsulated within TPU layers. The structure leverages the honeycomb’s high strength-to-weight ratio and the adaptive stiffening behavior of STF to reduce cleat-to-cleat impact forces while maintaining flexibility and minimal added mass. A dropweight test rig was designed to analyse impact force to optimize protection, manufacturability, and athletic performance.
